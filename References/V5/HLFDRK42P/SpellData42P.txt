Modifications to spell data (use maximize for best view)


The cost for Teleport, Fire Wall, Lightning Wall and Arcane Flames is increased.

The 'triple spell' scrolls appeared to have a bug that sometimes caused more then one scroll to be used upon casting. This is fixed by transforming those scrolls into runes. See also BaseItemData42P and RuneFixes.
